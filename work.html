My background includes also a year and a half as the video editor for a major YouTube channel with over 2.6 million subscribers. Now, I partner with creators and brands to produce impactful content across fashion, animation, events, and music videos.

My documentary experience focuses on editing and color grading, primarily collaborating with NGOs on projects across Africa to bring impactful, real-world stories to light.

My corporate and commercial work spans on-set production and post-production. I assist startups on set to bring their image videos to life, while also working as a motion designer to create engaging commercial content.

My motion graphics work ranges from handling long and complex music video projects to integrating VFX and animation into social reels.
