about

Hi, I'm Matteo, a video editor and filmmaker based in Zurich. My path to this career was a journey of discovery. During my university studies, I constantly looked for my professional calling. This led me to travel and work across Australia, Spain, and Southeast Asia, exploring diverse roles.

Eventually, 3 years ago, I transitioned my passion for shooting and editing into a career. I began as a freelancer, serving small clients, and quickly advanced to a full-time position as a video editor and motion graphic designer for a YouTube channel with 2.5 million subscribers.

While I specialise in video editing for social media, I am now transitioning to commercial video and documentaries. My dream is to collaborate with renowned brands and agencies.
